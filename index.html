What's this all about?

We're bringing hackathons to UP! Come join us on Saturday, July 26 at 10:00 am for 24 hours of coding!

Meals and snacks will be provided as well as a few chances to win some door prizes! Projects will be scored Sunday morning.

Tips

You may want to start looking into some web/mobile technologies to help familiarize your self.

  • Mobile development platforms such as phonegap
  • Google Map api or any other map apis
  • Web development platforms such as xampp,wamp,lamp,nodejs... etc to help you get your dev environment up and running fast
  • Databases such as sql,mysql,nosql...etc
